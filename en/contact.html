Quick answers

⚠️ Report
How to report an incorrect event?
Open the event page in the app and use the ⚠️ Report button. Our team reviews each report within 24h.
+ Submit
How to submit an event?
From the app, tap + Add an event and follow the steps. Your event is reviewed and published within 48h. Open app →
⏳ Publication
My event isn't published after 48h?
Check your spam, then contact us with the title and date of the event at hello@lotbo.app.
