The project

All events,
one place.

LOTBO connects event organizers with their local community, whether they are tourists, travelers or residents. Our interactive map allows everyone to discover what is happening around them, in real time, anywhere in the world.

The platform is open. Anyone can submit an event in 2 minutes. We believe local culture deserves to be visible, and that communities know better than anyone what is happening around them.

lòt bò
From Haitian Creole — "over there, on the other side". LOTBO is everything happening on the other side of your door.

"Kisa k'ap pase lotbo?" — What's happening over there?

Our values

What we believe in.

🌍
Local first
Every event has a story rooted in a place. We highlight what is happening near you, not what is generally popular.
🔓
Open to all
Organizing or discovering an event should not require an account, fees or permission. LOTBO is free and open.
🤝
Community trust
Events are submitted by the community and verified by our team. Anyone can flag incorrect information.

Haitian pride

Our history

How LOTBO was born.

May 5, 2026

Born in Petit-Goâve, Haiti 🇭🇹

Handgod Abraham launches LOTBO from Petit-Goâve. The first version of the map shows Haitian events. The name comes from the Creole "lòt bò" — over there, on the other side.

May — June 2026

Global expansion · 16 countries · 56+ cities

In a few weeks, LOTBO expands across the Caribbean, Europe, North America, Africa and Asia. Automatic scrapers enrich the database daily.

June 2026

DevExpo 2026 · 3,000+ events · Organizations

LOTBO presents its platform at DevExpo 2026. The database exceeds 3,000 events with Scan & Publish (AI), Organization spaces and a complete gamification system.

July 2026 — Ongoing

Wikimania Paris · Play Store · Coming soon...

Wikimedia partnership in progress, Google Play Store submission, and ongoing development of new community features.
